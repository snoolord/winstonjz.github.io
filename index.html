My recipe for my Japanese Curry

What you'll need!

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Chicken
  • Golden Curry Powder

Instructions

  1. Peel potatoes, carrots
  2. Cut chicken, potatoes, carrots, and onions
  3. Boil potatoes and carrots until soft
  4. When potatoes and carrots are soft, take off heat and put in curry powder
  5. Chicken is ready to go into the pot at this point
  6. Once the chicken is done, the curry is ready to serve
